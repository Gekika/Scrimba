Nyamachoma is a popular dish in Kenya, it is a Swahili word that means roasted meat. It is a dish that is loved by many Kenyans and is a must-try for anyone visiting Kenya. Nyamachoma is usually made from goat meat, but it can also be made from beef, chicken, or fish.
